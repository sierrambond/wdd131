Plant Care by Type

Welcome to the plant care section! Here you will find tips and tricks to keep your plants healthy and thriving.

Different Types of Plants

Knowing what type of care each plant needs is key to helping them thrive. Succulents, for example, require less water than ferns or pothos.

Soil Tips

Most plants thrive in well-draining soil, but some prefer moist environments. Leca (clay balls) can also be used for water-based growing.

Toggle Extra Tips

Use a moisture meter to help monitor over or under watering. Make sure pots have drainage holes!
