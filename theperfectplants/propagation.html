Propagation Tips and Tricks

Strategies

Propagation is the process of growing new plants from clippings, leaves, or stems. Water and soil methods both work depending on the plant.

African Violets

African Violets can be propagated through leaf cuttings with and without the stem. Grandma's kitchen is full of healthy violets thanks to her propagation station!
